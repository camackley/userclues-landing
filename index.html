Frequently Asked Questions

What is User Clues?

User Clues is an AI‑powered platform that helps you validate your product hypotheses, run lean experiments and collect actionable user insights without building a full product. It’s the fastest way to test demand and optimise your UX.

How does the 4‑week experiment work?

Once you join the waitlist, you'll receive instructions for creating your landing page and driving traffic to it. During the four‑week sprint you'll monitor visitor behaviour and conversion rates using our dashboard. At the end of the experiment you'll know whether your value proposition resonates with your audience.

What happens if I don't reach 5 % conversion?

If your signup rate is below 5 %, it suggests your message or segment needs adjustment. We’ll provide recommendations to refine your value proposition and help you retest until you find a stronger fit.

Will I need to write any code?

No coding is required. We provide a drag‑and‑drop landing page builder and built‑in analytics. You focus on your idea; we handle the technical details.
